My Journey

I recently graduated from Weber State University with a Bachelor’s in Applied Mathematics and minors in Computer Science and UX/UI Design. Currently, I am expanding my horizons in the Instructional Technology and Learning Sciences Master’s program at Utah State University.

My passion lies at the intersection of AI, data, and personalized learning. I enjoy utilizing programming languages like Python, C++, Java, and R, alongside Figma, to create solutions that are both technically sound and highly accessible.
